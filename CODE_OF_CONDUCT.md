# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any abusive or discriminatory language

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and public spaces when an individual is representing the project.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers via repository issues with the label `conduct`.
